About Us

Where science meets
compassion

Pearl Dental Studio was founded by Dr. Ananya Sharma, a graduate of AIIMS with over 15 years of experience in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. Her philosophy is simple — every patient deserves world-class care in a space that feels like a retreat, not a clinic.

Our practice combines cutting-edge technology with a patient-first approach. From digital X-rays to pain-free laser treatments, every detail is designed for your comfort and lasting results.
